Vividness of Visual Imagery Questionnaire (VVIQ)

üß† Important Disclaimer

This screening tool is for educational and self-awareness purposes only. It is not a diagnostic instrument. The VVIQ was developed by David Marks (1973) to assess individual differences in visual imagery vividness. This assessment can help you understand your visual imagination abilities.

Instructions

This questionnaire assesses the vividness of your visual mental imagery.

‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Close your eyes before imagining each scene!

For each item, close your eyes and try to visualize the described scene or person. Then rate how vivid your mental image is using the scale provided.

Rating Scale:

5 - Perfectly clear and vivid (as clear as actually seeing it)
4 - Clear and reasonably vivid
3 - Moderately clear and vivid
2 - Vague and dim
1 - No image at all (you only "know" you are thinking of the object)

There are no right or wrong answers. Some people naturally experience vivid mental imagery, while others experience little to none. Both are normal variations in human cognition.
